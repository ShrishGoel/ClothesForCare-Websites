About Us

Empowering Underprivileged Children Through Sports

At ClothesForCare, our mission is to address the lack of proper sports attire for underprivileged children in government schools of India. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to participate in physical activities and sports, as it promotes their physical health and instills essential values like teamwork and self-confidence.

Shrish Goel, Co-Founder

We source sports attire, including T-shirts, shorts, track pants, and more, in bulk and distribute them directly to government schools in need. By providing these children with the necessary sports clothing, we aim to remove barriers and create equal opportunities for them to engage in physical activities, develop their talents, and build a healthier future.
